Internship with Chegg+PoP posted 04.14.2014

Early February I applied to a contest hosted by Chegg and Pencils of Promise (PoP) for a chance to intern with them this upcoming July. When reading the description about the contest, I knew immediately that this opportunity aligned perfectly with my passions and future career goals. The internship is a 10-day long trip, one day in New York and the other nine in Guatemala. Participants will have the chance to experience and learn about the PoP operations both at their New York headquarters and an abroad location of service in Guatemala to further understand how to start and sustain a non-profit organization.

The contest consists of three preliminary rounds: a photo contest, a video contest, and a final interview to determine the eight individuals who will win an opportunity to intern with Chegg+PoP. Great news! I was recently notified a few days ago that I will be advancing the final rounds of the contest. No words can explain how overwhelmingly excited I am about this. And with that said, I want to thank you everyone who have supported me throughout this chapter of my life. My gratitude goes out to my family, friends and especially a huge thanks to my wonderful boyfriend, David Coven. This would not have been possible without your love and support.

Now, I wait anxiously once again for my phone to ring… Wish me luck on this interview :)
